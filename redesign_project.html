Project Description

Västra Götalandsregionen (VGR), the regional authority in western Sweden responsible for healthcare and wellness, developed an app called AntiStress. Our task was to evaluate the app and identify opportunities for improvement. Through user research, we examined the app’s usability, functionality, and overall user experience. Based on our findings, we provided design recommendations aimed at enhancing the app’s effectiveness in helping users manage stress and improve their well-being.

Identified problems

Inconsistent Navigation

Navigation elements were used inconsistently across screens, causing confusion and disrupting task flow.

Unclear System Feedback

Users often couldn’t tell whether actions were successful, as confirmations and visual cues were unclear or absent.

User uncertainty

First-time users frequently relied on trial and error, showing hesitation and uncertainty when attempting to complete key tasks
